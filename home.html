    Hello, I'm Babesie Taller, a second-year information technology student at Catanduanes State University. I'm a 19-year-old living in Sto.Domingo Virac, Catanduanes. As an IT student, I can attest that it may be challenging to balance all the activities, especially when using a laptop. I only use my cousin's laptop, and she needs it for business, so I can't always borrow it. It's difficult for me to study without technology, therefore I'm making a lot of effort to keep up with my studies. I want to learn and study web development because I'm still not familiar to some of it. I am aware that familiarizing myself with it could take some time, but I am eager to learn more and develop my own capacities since I really think that it is all about the process and trusting the process will help me accomplish my dreams and goals in life.
